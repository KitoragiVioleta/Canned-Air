Frequently Asked Questions

Is the can really filled with air?

Absolutely. Each can contains 100% authentic air collected from the specified location using specialized atmospheric equipment. The collection process is performed at designated sites, then sealed in a sterile environment to lock in the pristine atmosphere. Every batch is catalogued with collection date and location for authenticity verification.

Can I take it on an airplane?

Yes, Canned Air is TSA-approved for carry-on and checked luggage. Our sealed cans are pressurized safely and comply with all FAA and TSA regulations for air travel, making them ideal souvenirs and gifts for travelers worldwide.

How long does the air last?

When sealed, the atmosphere remains preserved indefinitely—potentially for decades. This makes Canned Air perfect for long-term collecting. If you choose to open your can, we recommend experiencing the air within the first few minutes for optimal sensory impact. Once opened, enjoy the atmosphere immediately for the truest experience.

Is this a collectible item?

Yes, absolutely. Each can includes a unique batch ID and verified collection date, making it a genuine collectible. Collectors worldwide display sealed cans as premium pieces or unseal them for a curated sensory experience. Limited-batch availability makes each edition increasingly rare and valuable over time.

Where is the air collected?

We carefully select iconic locations known for their distinctive atmospheric qualities: the Swiss Alps for pure alpine clarity, Tokyo's sakura parks for spring florals, Iceland's glacial valleys for pristine arctic air, the Canadian Rockies for wild mountain essence, the Sahara Desert for warm, ancient winds, and the Amazon Rainforest for untamed tropical richness. Each location is selected for its environmental uniqueness and sensory significance.
