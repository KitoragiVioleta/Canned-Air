Frequently Asked Questions

Is the can really filled with air?

Yes! Each can contains real air collected from the specified location. The air is captured using specialized atmospheric equipment and sealed in a sterile environment to preserve its authenticity.

Can I take it on an airplane?

Yes! Our cans are TSA-approved and can be taken on flights. They're sealed and pressurized safely for air travel, making them perfect souvenirs or gifts.

How long does the air last?

The sealed can preserves the air indefinitely. Once opened, we recommend enjoying the experience within a few moments for the full sensory impact!

Is this a collectible item?

Absolutely! Each can comes with a unique batch ID and collection date. Many customers keep their cans sealed as collectibles, display pieces, or conversation starters.

Where is the air collected?

We collect air from iconic locations around the world, including the Swiss Alps, Tokyo's sakura parks, Iceland's glaciers, the Canadian Rockies, the Sahara Desert, and the Amazon Rainforest. Each location is carefully selected for its unique atmospheric qualities.
